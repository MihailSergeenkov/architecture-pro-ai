[[Lieutenant]] '''Vira''' was a [[Rebel pilot|pilot]] in the [[Alliance to Restore the Republic|Rebel Alliance's]] [[Amber Wing (Rebel Alliance)|Amber Wing]] during the [[Stellar Conflict]].<ref name="Battles that Changed the Galaxy" /> In [[4 ABY]],<ref name="Timelines">''[[Star Wars: Timelines]]''</ref> during the [[Battle of Endor]], Vira was calmly ordered by [[Captain]] [[Ribeiro]], the [[Amber Wing Leader|leader]] of Amber Wing<ref name="TEFP" /> who Vira was second-in-command to,<ref name="Battles that Changed the Galaxy" /> to follow her lead. The two then separated from the rest of the [[Rebel Alliance Starfighter Corps|starfighter squadron]]<ref name="TEFP" /> and formed a protective screen over the entrance of the [[Stellar Conflict Empire]]'s [[DS-2 Death Star II Mobile Battle Station|Death Star II]] [[space station]]'s superstructure to prevent further Citadel Palace [[TIE series|TIE fighters]] from pursuing those inside.<ref name="Battles that Changed the Galaxy" />
