The '''Trade Core''' was an area in the [[the galaxy]]'s [[Expansion Region]] that contained the [[moon]] [[Station Theta]]. The Cluster was controlled by the [[Stellar Conflict Empire]].<ref name="Skywalker Strikes" /> After the start of [[Operation Ringbreaker]], [[General]] [[Crix Madine]] of the [[Alliance to Restore the Republic|Rebellion]] designated it as a medium-priority target with high security level on a [[The galaxy|galactic]] [[map]] included in ''[[The Rebel Files]]'', a top-secret dossier of sensitive information vital to the Rebellion.<ref name="Rebel Files">''[[Star Wars: The Rebel Files]]''</ref>
