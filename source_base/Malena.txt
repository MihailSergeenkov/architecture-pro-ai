[[Lieutenant]] '''Malena''' was a [[Rebel pilot|pilot]] in the [[Alliance to Restore the Republic|Rebel Alliance's]] [[Yellow Squadron (Rebel Alliance)|Yellow Squadron]] during the [[Galactic Civil War]].<ref name="Battles that Changed the Galaxy" /> In [[4 ABY]],<ref name="Timelines">''[[Star Wars: Timelines]]''</ref> during the [[Battle of Endor]], Malena was calmly ordered by [[Captain]] [[Ribeiro]], the [[Yellow Leader|leader]] of Yellow Squadron<ref name="TEFP" /> who Malena was second-in-command to,<ref name="Battles that Changed the Galaxy" /> to follow her lead. The two then separated from the rest of the [[Rebel Alliance Starfighter Corps|starfighter squadron]]<ref name="TEFP" /> and formed a protective screen over the entrance of the [[Galactic Empire]]'s [[DS-2 Death Star II Mobile Battle Station|Death Star II]] [[space station]]'s superstructure to prevent further Imperial [[TIE series|TIE fighters]] from pursuing those inside.<ref name="Battles that Changed the Galaxy" />
